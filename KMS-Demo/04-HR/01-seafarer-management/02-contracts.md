# Seafarer Employment Agreement (SEA)

## Purpose

This document describes the Seafarer Employment Agreement as required by the Maritime Labour Convention (MLC 2006).

---

## MLC Requirements

The MLC requires that all seafarers have a written employment agreement that includes:

| Required Element | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| Full name, birthdate | Seafarer identification |
| Employer name/address | Company information |
| Place & date of agreement | Where signed |
| Capacity | Position (rank) |
| Wages | Amount and payment schedule |
| Leave entitlement | Paid annual leave |
| Termination | Notice period, conditions |
| Health/social security | Coverage provided |
| Repatriation | Return home provisions |

---

## Contract Types

### Fixed-Term Contract

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Duration** | Typically 3-12 months |
| **Common for** | Most seafarer positions |
| **Renewal** | Subject to performance |

### Permanent Contract

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Duration** | Ongoing |
| **Common for** | Senior officers, key personnel |
| **Benefits** | Enhanced job security |

---

## Contract Process

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | HR prepares SEA |
| 2 | Seafarer reviews terms |
| 3 | Seafarer may consult before signing |
| 4 | Both parties sign |
| 5 | Seafarer receives copy |
| 6 | Original retained by company |

---

## Key Terms

### Wages

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Basic wage | Monthly salary |
| Leave pay | Accrued leave component |
| Overtime | As per agreement |
| Allowances | Position-specific |

### Work/Rest Hours

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| Minimum rest | 10 hours in 24-hour period |
| Weekly rest | 77 hours in 7-day period |
| Records | Work/rest hours recorded |

### Leave

| Type | Entitlement |
|------|-------------|
| Annual leave | Minimum 2.5 days per month |
| Home leave | After contract period |
| Sick leave | As required, with medical |

### Repatriation

| Circumstance | Entitlement |
|--------------|-------------|
| Contract expiry | Return to place of engagement |
| Medical | If unfit for duty |
| Termination | Per terms |

---

## Termination

| Party | Notice Required |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Company** | Per contract terms |
| **Seafarer** | Per contract terms |
| **Summary dismissal** | For gross misconduct |
