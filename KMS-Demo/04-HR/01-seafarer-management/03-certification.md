# Seafarer Certification

## Purpose

This document outlines certification requirements for seafarers employed by IMC Pelita Logistik.

---

## STCW Framework

All seafarer certifications are governed by the International Convention on Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping (STCW).

---

## Certificate Types

### Certificate of Competency (CoC)

| Purpose | National license to serve in rank |
|---------|-----------------------------------|
| Issued by | Flag State (Indonesia) |
| Validity | 5 years |
| Renewal | Revalidation required |

### STCW Endorsement

| Purpose | Flag state recognition of CoC |
|---------|-------------------------------|
| Required | For all officers |
| Validity | Matches CoC validity |

### Proficiency Certificates

| Certificate | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| Basic Safety Training (BST) | All seafarers |
| Advanced Firefighting | Officers, designated crew |
| Medical First Aid | Officers |
| GMDSS (GOC/ROC) | Radio officers, Masters |
| Survival Craft | Designated lifeboat crew |

---

## Requirements by Position

### Deck Officers

| Position | CoC Required |
|----------|--------------|
| Master | Master Mariner |
| Chief Officer | Chief Mate |
| 2nd/3rd Officer | OOW Deck |

### Engineer Officers

| Position | CoC Required |
|----------|--------------|
| Chief Engineer | Chief Engineer |
| 2nd Engineer | 2nd Engineer |
| 3rd/4th Engineer | OOW Engine |

### Ratings

| Position | Certificate |
|----------|-------------|
| AB | STCW II/5 |
| Oiler | STCW III/5 |
| All ratings | BST |

## Company-Specific Requirements

Beyond STCW:

| Requirement | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| Company induction | Company procedures |
| Vessel familiarization | Equipment-specific |
| Coal cargo training | Cargo hazards |
| Role-specific training | As per training matrix |

## Certification Management

### Tracking

| Item | System |
|------|--------|
| Certificate database | Crew management system |
| Expiry monitoring | Automated alerts |
| Renewal planning | 3 months before expiry |

### Verification

| Action | Timing |
|--------|--------|
| Check authenticity | On joining |
| Verify with issuing authority | For new hires |
| Copy retained | Company records |

### Renewal Support

| Support | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Advance notice | 3 months before expiry |
| Leave for courses | Arranged as needed |
| Course costs | Per company policy |

---

## Flag State Requirements

For Indonesian-flagged vessels:
- Indonesian CoC or recognized foreign CoC
- Indonesian endorsement required
- Issued by Direktorat Jenderal Perhubungan Laut
