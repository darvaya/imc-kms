# Seafarer Recruitment

## Purpose

This document describes the recruitment process for seafarers joining IMC Pelita Logistik.

---

## Recruitment Principles

| Principle | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Qualification** | STCW-certified candidates only |
| **Experience** | Preference for relevant experience |
| **Medical fitness** | Valid medical certificate required |
| **Background check** | Verify credentials and history |

---

## Recruitment Process

### Step 1: Application

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Website | Online application portal |
| Agency | Approved manning agencies |
| Referral | Employee referrals welcome |
| Walk-in | Direct applications |

### Step 2: Screening

| Check | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Document verification | CoC, endorsements, medical |
| Experience review | Previous sea service |
| Background check | References, previous employers |

### Step 3: Interview

| Stage | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Technical interview** | Assess competency |
| **HR interview** | Cultural fit, expectations |
| **Medical examination** | Fitness for duty |

### Step 4: Offer & Onboarding

| Activity | Timing |
|----------|--------|
| Employment offer | After successful interview |
| Contract signing | Before joining vessel |
| Company induction | Before first assignment |
| Vessel familiarization | On joining |

---

## Qualification Requirements

### Officers

| Position | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| Master | Appropriate CoC + endorsement |
| Chief Officer | Appropriate CoC + endorsement |
| Chief Engineer | Appropriate CoC + endorsement |
| 2nd/3rd Officers | Appropriate CoC + endorsement |

### Ratings

| Position | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| AB/OS | Rating certificate |
| Oiler | Engine room rating |
| Cook | Basic food hygiene |

### All Seafarers

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| STCW Basic Safety | BST, PSC, survival |
| Medical | Valid PEME |
| Flag State endorsement | Indonesian or equivalent |

---

## Manning Agencies

For positions filled through agencies:
- Use approved agencies only
- Verify agency MLC compliance
- Maintain agency performance records
