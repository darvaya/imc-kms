# Grievance Procedure

## Purpose

This document describes the procedure for employees to raise complaints or grievances.

---

## Principles

| Principle | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Accessibility** | All employees can raise grievances |
| **Fairness** | Impartial investigation |
| **Timeliness** | Prompt handling |
| **Confidentiality** | Maintained where possible |
| **No retaliation** | Protection from victimization |

---

## Scope

This procedure covers complaints about:
- Working conditions
- Treatment by colleagues or supervisors
- Application of policies
- Other employment-related concerns

> For safety concerns, use the safety reporting channel.

---

## Procedure

### Step 1: Informal Resolution

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Discuss** | Raise issue with supervisor |
| **Timeline** | As soon as possible |
| **Goal** | Quick resolution |

### Step 2: Formal Grievance

If informal resolution fails:

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Submit** | Written grievance to HR |
| **Include** | Nature of complaint, facts, resolution sought |
| **Timeline** | Within reasonable time |

### Step 3: Investigation

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Review** | HR investigates |
| **Interview** | Speak to parties involved |
| **Evidence** | Gather relevant information |
| **Timeline** | Aim to complete within 14 days |

### Step 4: Decision

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Outcome** | Written decision |
| **Communicate** | To complainant |
| **Action** | Remedial measures if needed |

### Step 5: Appeal

| Action | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Right** | Appeal within 7 days |
| **To** | Higher management |
| **Final** | Appeal decision is final |

---

## For Seafarers

MLC requires on-board grievance procedures:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Raise with Head of Department |
| 2 | Escalate to Master if unresolved |
| 3 | Escalate to shore (DPA) if unresolved |
