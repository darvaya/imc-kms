# Employee Benefits

## Purpose

This document outlines benefits provided to employees.

---

## Seafarer Benefits

### Health & Medical

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Medical treatment** | Covered while on contract |
| **Sick wages** | Per MLC requirements |
| **Repatriation** | If medically unfit |
| **Death/disability** | Compensation per law/contract |

### Leave

| Benefit | Entitlement |
|---------|-------------|
| **Annual leave** | Min 2.5 days per month |
| **Home leave** | After contract period |
| **Sick leave** | As required |

### On-board Welfare

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Accommodation** | Per vessel standards |
| **Food** | Provided on-board |
| **Communication** | Access to call home |
| **Recreation** | Common areas, entertainment |

## BPJS Enrollment

All eligible employees are enrolled in:
- BPJS Kesehatan (health insurance)
- BPJS Ketenagakerjaan (employment benefits)

| Program | Coverage |
|---------|----------|
| **Jaminan Kecelakaan Kerja (JKK)** | Work accident |
| **Jaminan Kematian (JKm)** | Death benefit |
| **Jaminan Hari Tua (JHT)** | Old age savings |
| **Jaminan Pensiun (JP)** | Pension |
