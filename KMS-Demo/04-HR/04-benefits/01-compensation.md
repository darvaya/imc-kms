# Compensation

## Purpose

This document outlines the compensation framework for employees.

---

## Compensation Philosophy

IMC Pelita Logistik aims to provide:
- Competitive compensation
- Fair and transparent pay practices
- Alignment with performance

---

## Seafarer Compensation

### Wage Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Basic wage** | Monthly salary per contract |
| **Leave pay** | Accrued leave entitlement |
| **Overtime** | Per applicable rates |
| **Allowances** | Position/role-specific |

### Payment

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Frequency** | Monthly |
| **Method** | Bank transfer |
| **Currency** | IDR (or per contract) |
| **Allotment** | Family allotment available |
