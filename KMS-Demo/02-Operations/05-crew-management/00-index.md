## Crew Organization

### Shore-based

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Crewing Manager** | Overall crewing management |
| **Manning Officer** | Recruitment, rotation |
| **Training Coordinator** | Training management |

### On-board

| Department | Key Roles |
|------------|-----------|
| **Deck** | Master, Officers, Ratings |
| **Engine** | Chief Engineer, Officers, Ratings |
| **Catering** | Cook, Steward |
| **Cargo (FLF)** | Crane operators, Conveyor operators |

---

## Key Principles

| Principle | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Qualified** | STCW-certified crew only |
| **Medically fit** | Valid medical certificates |
| **Well-rested** | STCW work/rest compliance |
| **Trained** | Company-specific training |
| **Supported** | Access to resources and management |
