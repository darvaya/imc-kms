# Crew Rotation

## Purpose

This document describes crew rotation schedules and handover procedures.

---

## Rotation Schedule

| Vessel Type | Contract Period | Leave Period |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| FLF | 2-3 months | 1 month |
| Tug | 2-3 months | 1 month |
| MV (domestic) | 3 months | 1 month |

> **Note:** Actual periods depend on operational requirements and agreements.

## Handover Procedure

### Pre-Handover

| Outgoing | Incoming |
|----------|----------|
| Prepare handover notes | Review vessel information |
| Update PMS records | Check certificates ready |
| Outstanding issues list | Travel arrangements |

### During Handover

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Overlap** | Min 4-24 hours | Joint walkthrough |
| **Briefing** | 1-2 hours | Status handover |
| **Tour** | 1-2 hours | Equipment familiarization |
| **Documentation** | As needed | Sign-off on records |

### Handover Contents

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Operational status** | Current operations, cargo |
| **Equipment status** | Defects, running hours |
| **Outstanding work** | Pending tasks |
| **Class/surveys** | Upcoming requirements |
| **Crew issues** | Any personnel matters |
| **Stores status** | Fuel, water, provisions |
| **Documentation** | Keys, safe contents |

|------|---------|
