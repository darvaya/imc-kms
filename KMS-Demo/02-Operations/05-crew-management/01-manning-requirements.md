# Manning Requirements

## Purpose

This document outlines safe manning requirements for IMC Pelita Logistik vessels.

---

## Safe Manning Requirements

Every vessel must carry a Minimum Safe Manning Document (MSMD) issued by the Flag State, specifying the minimum crew required.

### Legal Basis

| Regulation | Requirement |
|------------|-------------|
| SOLAS | Safe manning |
| STCW | Crew qualifications |
| Flag State | Minimum manning certificate |

---

### Barge

Barges are typically unmanned – crew provided by attending tug.

## Qualification Requirements

### Officers

| Position | Minimum Certificate |
|----------|---------------------|
| Master | CoC for vessel tonnage |
| Chief Officer | CoC for vessel tonnage |
| Chief Engineer | CoC for engine power |
| 2nd Engineer | CoC for engine power |
| Radio Officer/GMDSS | GOC or ROC |

### Ratings

| Position | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| AB | Rating certificate |
| Oiler | Engine room rating |
| Other | Basic safety training |

---

## Additional Requirements

### Mandatory Certificates

| Certificate | Valid |
|-------------|-------|
| CoC | Not expired |
| GMDSS | If required |
| Medical | Current |
| STCW Basic Safety | Refreshed if required |

### Company Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Company induction | Before first assignment |
| Vessel familiarization | On joining |
| Specific training | As per role |

| Factor | Consideration |
