# Onboard Familiarization

## Purpose

This document describes the familiarization process for crew members joining a vessel.

---

## Legal Requirements

| Regulation | Requirement |
|------------|-------------|
| **ISM Code** | Familiarization before duties |
| **STCW** | Proper handover and familiarization |
| **SOLAS** | Emergency duties briefing |

---

## Familiarization Types

| Type | When | Duration |
|------|------|----------|
| **New joiner** | First time on this vessel | Full program |
| **Re-joiner** | Returning after absence | Abbreviated |
| **Same vessel** | Rotation on same vessel | Brief update |

---

## Familiarization Program

### On Arrival

| Time | Activity | Responsibility |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **First hour** | Welcome, cabin assignment | Relief officer |
| **First hour** | Emergency duties card | Safety officer |
| **First 4 hours** | Safety familiarization | Safety officer |

### Safety Familiarization

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Emergency stations** | Muster station, duties |
| **Lifesaving appliances** | Location, use |
| **Fire-fighting** | Equipment, alarm system |
| **Emergency escape** | Routes, assembly points |
| **Personal safety** | PPE, specific hazards |
| **Pollution prevention** | SOPEP, procedures |
| **Communication** | Emergency signals |

### First 24 Hours

| Activity | Covered By |
|----------|------------|
| Department tour | Head of department |
| Equipment orientation | Supervisor |
| Watch duties briefing | Outgoing counterpart |
| SMS overview | Master/Safety Officer |

### First Week

| Activity | Covered |
|----------|---------|
| Ship-specific procedures | Detailed review |
| Equipment operation | Hands-on training |
| Drills participation | At least one |
| Confirmation | Familiarization signed off |
