# Fleet Management

This section provides an overview of IMC Pelita Logistik's fleet and vessel-specific operational procedures.

---

## Fleet Composition

IMC Pelita Logistik operates a diverse fleet purpose-built for coal transshipment:

| Vessel Type | Quantity | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| **Floating Loading Facility (FLF)** | Multiple | Transshipment to MVs |
| **Tugs** | Multiple | Barge towing |
| **Barges** | Multiple | Coal transport |
| **Motor Vessels** | As needed | Ocean transport |

## Fleet Management Principles

1. **Safety First** – All operations prioritize crew and vessel safety
2. **Reliability** – Maintain high vessel availability
3. **Efficiency** – Optimize fleet utilization
4. **Compliance** – Meet all regulatory requirements
5. **Environmental** – Minimize environmental impact
