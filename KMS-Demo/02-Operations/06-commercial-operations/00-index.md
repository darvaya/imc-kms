# Commercial Operations

This section covers the commercial aspects of operations including chartering, performance metrics, and customer requirements.

---

## Business Model

IMC Pelita Logistik provides integrated coal transshipment services:

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Barge transport** | River transport from jetty to anchorage |
| **Transshipment** | FLF loading to MVs |
| **Marine services** | Tug assist, mooring |
