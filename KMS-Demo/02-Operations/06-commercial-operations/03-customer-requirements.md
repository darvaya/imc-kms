# Customer Requirements

## Purpose

This document outlines general customer requirements and how to access customer-specific procedures.

---

## Customer Focus

IMC Pelita Logistik is committed to meeting our customers' requirements for:
- Quality service delivery
- Timely operations
- Safety standards
- Environmental responsibility
- Documentation and reporting

---

## Common Customer Requirements

### Quality

| Requirement | IMC Response |
|-------------|--------------|
| **Cargo quality** | Minimize handling, contamination |
| **Sampling** | Support independent surveyors |
| **Documentation** | Accurate, timely |

### Safety

| Requirement | IMC Response |
|-------------|--------------|
| **ISM compliance** | Valid DOC/SMC |
| **Vetting** | Pass RISQ/Rightship |
| **Incident reporting** | Immediate notification |
| **PPE** | All visitors wear PPE |

### Environmental

| Requirement | IMC Response |
|-------------|--------------|
| **Dust control** | Water spray, minimize spillage |
| **No pollution** | Spill prevention |
| **Waste management** | Proper disposal |

### Operational

| Requirement | IMC Response |
|-------------|--------------|
| **Loading rate** | Meet contracted rate |
| **Reporting** | Daily/voyage reports |
| **Communication** | 24/7 contact |

---

## Customer-Specific Procedures

Some customers have specific requirements that go beyond standard procedures. These may include:

| Area | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **Safety** | Additional PPE, training |
| **Operations** | Loading rate targets |
| **Documentation** | Specific forms, reports |
| **Inspections** | Regular audits |

> **Note:** Customer-specific procedures are maintained separately and shared with relevant vessels.
