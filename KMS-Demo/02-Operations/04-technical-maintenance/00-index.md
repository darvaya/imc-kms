# Technical & Maintenance

This section covers technical management, planned maintenance, and classification requirements for the fleet.

---

## Technical Management Principles

1. **Reliability** – Maintain high vessel availability
2. **Compliance** – Meet all regulatory requirements
3. **Planned maintenance** – Prevent failures through maintenance
4. **Cost-effective** – Balance quality and cost
5. **Continuous improvement** – Learn from defects and failures
