# Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines acceptable use of company IT resources.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees (sea and shore)
- Contractors and consultants
- Visitors using company IT

---

## Company IT Resources

| Resource | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Hardware** | Computers, phones, tablets |
| **Networks** | Office, vessel, WiFi |
| **Software** | Business applications |
| **Email** | Company email accounts |
| **Internet** | Network access |
| **Data** | Company information |

---

## Acceptable Use

### General Principles

| Principle | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Business purpose** | Primarily for business use |
| **Professional conduct** | Act professionally |
| **Respect** | Respect others |
| **Compliance** | Follow all policies |

### Permitted Activities

| Activity | Guidelines |
|----------|------------|
| **Email** | Business communication |
| **Internet** | Work-related browsing |
| **Personal use** | Limited, reasonable |
| **Software** | Approved applications only |

---

## Prohibited Activities

| Prohibited | Examples |
|------------|----------|
| **Illegal content** | Piracy, pornography |
| **Harassment** | Offensive content |
| **Unauthorized software** | Unapproved installations |
| **Sharing credentials** | Password sharing |
| **Bypassing security** | Circumventing controls |
| **Excessive personal use** | Interfering with work |

---

## Email Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Professional tone | Offensive content |
| Clear subject lines | Confidential data unencrypted |
| Business use | Chain emails, spam |
| Verify before sending | Reply-all unnecessarily |

---

## Password Requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Length** | Minimum 8 characters |
| **Complexity** | Mixed case, numbers, symbols |
| **Change** | Every 90 days |
| **Unique** | Don't reuse passwords |
| **Don't share** | Never share passwords |

---

## Monitoring

The company reserves the right to monitor IT resource usage for security and compliance purposes.

---

## Violations

Violations may result in:
- Disciplinary action
- Loss of access
- Termination
