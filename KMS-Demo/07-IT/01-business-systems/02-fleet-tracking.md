# Fleet Tracking

## Purpose

This document describes the fleet tracking and monitoring systems.

---

## Overview

Real-time vessel tracking enables:
- Position monitoring
- Voyage progress tracking
- Performance analysis
- Safety monitoring

---

## Tracking Technology

### AIS (Automatic Identification System)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **What** | Maritime identification system |
| **Transmits** | Position, course, speed, vessel info |
| **Range** | VHF range (~20-40 nm) |
| **Requirement** | SOLAS mandatory |

### Satellite Tracking

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **What** | Global satellite positioning |
| **Coverage** | Worldwide |
| **Features** | Position, speed, heading |
| **Reporting** | Configurable intervals |
