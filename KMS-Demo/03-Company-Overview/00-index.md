# About IMC Pelita Logistik

Welcome to the Company Overview section of the IMC Pelita Logistik Knowledge Management System.

## Who We Are

**PT IMC Pelita Logistik Tbk** (IDX: PSSI) is a leading Indonesian maritime logistics company specializing in coal transshipment and integrated logistics solutions.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Legal Name** | PT IMC Pelita Logistik Tbk |
| **Stock Code** | PSSI (Indonesia Stock Exchange) |
| **Headquarters** | Jakarta, Indonesia |
| **Founded** | 2007 (as PT Pelita Samudera Shipping Tbk) |
| **Rebranded** | February 2023 |
| **Parent Company** | IMC Industrial Group |
