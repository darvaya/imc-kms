# Organizational Structure

## Overview

PT IMC Pelita Logistik Tbk follows the dual-board governance structure mandated by Indonesian corporate law:

- **Board of Commissioners** – Oversight and governance
- **Board of Directors** – Executive management

## Board Committees

### Audit Committee
- Oversees financial reporting
- Internal controls
- External audit relationship

### Nomination & Remuneration Committee
- Board composition
- Executive compensation
- Succession planning

## Operational Structure

| Division | Responsibility |
|----------|----------------|
| **Operations** | Fleet management, vessel operations |
| **Technical** | Maintenance, dry dock, class |
| **Commercial** | Chartering, customer relations |
| **Finance** | Accounting, treasury, reporting |
| **HSSE** | Safety, health, security, environment |
| **Human Resources** | Crewing, shore staff, training |
| **Corporate Affairs** | Governance, investor relations |
