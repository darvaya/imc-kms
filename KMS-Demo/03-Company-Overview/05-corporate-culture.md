# Corporate Culture – COMPASS

## The COMPASS Framework

IMC Pelita Logistik has institutionalized a robust corporate culture framework known as **COMPASS**. This framework guides employee behavior and decision-making across all operations.

---

## The Values

### C – Collaboration

> **"Thinking and working together; expressing different ideas to reach agreements."**

| Application | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Operations** | Coordinating complex transshipment with multiple stakeholders |
| **Safety** | Team-based hazard identification and mitigation |
| **Growth** | Cross-functional project teams |

---

### O – Mindfulness (Observant)

> **"Consistently considering the impact of decisions and behaviors on others and the environment."**

| Application | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Safety** | Awareness of surroundings and hazards |
| **Environment** | Minimizing impact on river ecosystems |
| **Colleagues** | Respect for multicultural crew |

---

### M – Prudence (Managing Resources)

> **"Taking responsibility for actions, learning from mistakes, and managing resources effectively."**

| Application | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Financial** | Cost control in capital-intensive operations |
| **Time** | Efficient scheduling and turnaround |
| **Assets** | Proper maintenance and care of vessels |

---

### P – Add Value (Pioneer)

> **"Abandoning old ways of thinking to adopt novel solutions; intrinsic motivation driven by challenge."**

| Application | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Innovation** | New loading techniques |
| **Problem-solving** | Creative solutions to operational challenges |
| **Improvement** | Continuous process enhancement |

---

### A – Strategic Mindset (Agile)

> **"Proactively adjusting actions in response to changing needs; deploying resources effectively."**

| Application | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Flexibility** | Pivoting assets between routes as market shifts |
| **Responsiveness** | Quick response to customer needs |
| **Adaptation** | Adjusting to regulatory changes |

---

### S – Stewardship

> **"Serving with dedication and protecting the company's assets and reputation."**

| Application | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **Duty** | Fiduciary responsibility to shareholders |
| **Care** | Maintenance of high-value fleet assets |
| **Reputation** | Upholding company standards |

---

## Living COMPASS

| Situation | COMPASS Value |
|-----------|---------------|
| Safety meeting | **Collaboration** – team input |
| Environmental spill prevention | **Mindfulness** – environmental awareness |
| Budget management | **Prudence** – resource management |
| Process improvement | **Add Value** – innovation |
| Market downturn response | **Strategic Mindset** – adaptation |
| Asset care | **Stewardship** – protection |

---

## Recognition

Employees who exemplify COMPASS values are recognized through:
- Team meetings
- Performance reviews
- Awards and ceremonies
