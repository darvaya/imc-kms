# Our Fleet

## Fleet Overview

IMC Pelita Logistik operates a diverse fleet of over 60 vessels purpose-built for coal transshipment and maritime logistics.

---

## Fleet Composition

| Vessel Type | Quantity | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| **Floating Loading Facilities (FLF)** | 2 | Offshore transshipment |
| **Tugboats** | 31 | Barge towing |
| **Barges** | 26 | Coal transport |
| **Motor Vessels** | 5 | Ocean transport |

**Total: 64 vessels**

---

## Floating Loading Facilities (FLF)

Our FLFs are purpose-built transshipment platforms:

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Function** | Transfer coal from barges to MVs |
| **Loading rate** | 1,500-3,000 tonnes/hour |
| **Equipment** | Cranes, conveyors, loading boom |
| **Location** | Anchorage areas |

> **Strategic Advantage:** FLFs are critical infrastructure bottlenecks. By controlling this node, we secure long-term relationships with major miners.

---

## Tugboats

Our tugs provide motive power for barge operations:

| Type | Quantity | Bollard Pull | Use |
|------|----------|--------------|-----|
| **River tugs** | Majority | 25-50 tonnes | River/anchorage |
| **Ocean tugs** | Some | 50-80 tonnes | Long-distance |

---

## Barges

Flat-deck bulk barges for coal transport:

| Size Category | Capacity |
|---------------|----------|
| **Standard** | 8,000-10,000 DWT |
| **Large** | 10,000-12,000 DWT |

---

## Motor Vessels

Ocean-going bulk carriers for export:

| Class | Capacity |
|-------|----------|
| **Supramax/Handymax** | 50,000+ DWT |

These vessels transport coal, nickel, clinker, and iron sand to China, India, the Philippines, and Vietnam.
