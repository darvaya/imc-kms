## Maritime Certifications

### ISM Code – International Safety Management

| Certificate | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Document of Compliance (DOC)** | Company-level certification |
| **Safety Management Certificate (SMC)** | Vessel-level certification |

The ISM Code ensures:
- Safe ship operation
- Prevention of human injury
- Avoidance of environmental damage

### Class Certificates

All vessels are classed with recognized classification societies:

| Certificate | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **Class Certificate** | Hull and machinery |
| **Loadline Certificate** | Freeboard and safety |
| **Safety Equipment Certificate** | LSA/FFA |
| **IOPP Certificate** | Oil pollution prevention |
