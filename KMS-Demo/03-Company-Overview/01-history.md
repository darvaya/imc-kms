# Company History

## Our Journey

PT IMC Pelita Logistik Tbk has grown from a small domestic shipping operator to a leading maritime logistics company integrated with a global industrial group.

---

## Timeline

### 2007 – Foundation
- Company founded as **PT Pelita Samudera Shipping Tbk**
- Initial focus on coal transportation in Kalimantan
- Established relationships with major coal miners

### 2007–2015 – Growth Phase
- Built reputation as reliable logistics partner
- Rapid fleet expansion (tugs and barges)
- Established "Pelita" brand in domestic coal transport
- Key partnerships with PT Adaro Indonesia and other major miners

### 2015–2020 – Expansion
- Acquired Floating Loading Facilities (FLF)
- Added Motor Vessels for international operations
- Diversified cargo base beyond coal
- Strengthened operational excellence

### 2020–2022 – Peak Performance
- **2022**: Record profitability during commodity super-cycle
- Revenue: $119.3 million
- Net profit: $42.3 million
- ROE: 29%

### 2022–2023 – Strategic Transformation
- **December 2022**: Shareholder approval for rebranding
- **February 14, 2023**: Official name change to **PT IMC Pelita Logistik Tbk**
- Integration with IMC Industrial Group brand identity
- Adoption of the "Red Diamond" visual identity

### 2024–Present – Resilience & Optimization
- Fleet rationalization and optimization
- Focus on operational efficiency
- Maintained strong balance sheet
- Continued dividend payments to shareholders

---

## The Rebranding Story

### Why We Rebranded

The transformation from "Pelita Samudera Shipping" to "IMC Pelita Logistik" reflected:

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Evolution** | From shipping operator to logistics solutions provider |
| **Integration** | Alignment with IMC Industrial Group |
| **Market access** | Leverage IMC brand in international markets |
| **Diversification** | Beyond pure-play coal transportation |

### The New Identity

Our new visual identity features the iconic **Red Diamond** of IMC Shipping Management:

- 🔴 **Red ring** – Holistic, end-to-end logistics
- ➡️ **Traversing lines** – Movement, speed, seamless transfer
- ◆ **Diamond shape** – Strength, value, precision
