# Customer Relations

## Purpose

This document covers customer relationship management practices.

---

## Relationship Management

### Key Account Management

Major customers have designated account managers:

| Responsibility | Action |
|----------------|--------|
| **Single point of contact** | Coordinate internally |
| **Relationship building** | Regular engagement |
| **Issue resolution** | Escalation point |
| **Growth** | Identify opportunities |

---

## Customer Communication

### Regular Touchpoints

| Touchpoint | Frequency |
|------------|-----------|
| Operational updates | As needed |
| Performance reviews | Quarterly |
| Strategic meetings | Annual |
| Issue resolution | Immediate |

### Communication Channels

| Channel | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Email | Formal communication |
| Phone | Urgent matters |
| Meetings | Reviews, negotiations |
| Reports | Performance data |

---

## Customer Feedback

| Method | Frequency |
|--------|-----------|
| Performance discussions | Per voyage |
| Formal surveys | Annual |
| Complaint handling | As received |

### Feedback Loop

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Collect feedback |
| 2 | Analyze and categorize |
| 3 | Develop action plan |
| 4 | Implement improvements |
| 5 | Communicate back |
