# Anti-Corruption Policy

## Purpose

IMC Pelita Logistik has zero tolerance for bribery and corruption in any form.

---

## Policy Statement

We are committed to:
- Conducting business ethically
- Compliance with anti-corruption laws
- Zero tolerance for bribery
- Transparency in all dealings

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees (sea and shore)
- Directors and commissioners
- Agents and representatives
- Business partners

---

## Prohibited Conduct

### Bribery

| Prohibited | Examples |
|------------|----------|
| **Giving bribes** | Payments to officials for advantage |
| **Receiving bribes** | Accepting payments for favors |
| **Facilitation payments** | Payments to speed routine processes |

### Other Prohibited Conduct

| Prohibited | Examples |
|------------|----------|
| **Kickbacks** | Payments for contract awards |
| **Fraud** | Falsifying records |
| **Conflicts of interest** | Undisclosed personal interests |

---

## Gifts & Hospitality

| Allowed | Not Allowed |
|---------|-------------|
| Modest gifts (token value) | Lavish gifts |
| Business meals (reasonable) | Excessive entertainment |
| Promotional items | Cash or cash equivalents |

### Approval Required

| Value | Approval |
|-------|----------|
| Under IDR 500,000 | Department head |
| IDR 500,000 - 2,000,000 | Director |
| Above IDR 2,000,000 | President Director |

---

## Government Officials

Extra caution required when dealing with government officials:
- Never offer anything of value for business advantage
- All entertainment must be modest and disclosed
- Any request for payment must be refused and reported

---

## Reporting Violations

| Channel | Use |
|---------|-----|
| **Supervisor** | First point of contact |
| **Compliance** | Policy questions |
| **Confidential hotline** | Anonymous reporting |

### Whistleblower Protection

Persons reporting in good faith are protected from retaliation.

---

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Disciplinary action
- Termination
- Legal action
- Regulatory penalties
