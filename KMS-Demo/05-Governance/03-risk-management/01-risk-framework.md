## Risk Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Strategic** | Market, competition, reputation |
| **Operational** | Fleet, cargo, safety |
| **Financial** | Credit, liquidity, forex |
| **Compliance** | Regulatory, legal |
| **External** | Economic, political, environmental |

---

## Risk Assessment

### Likelihood

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **5 - Almost Certain** | Expected to occur |
| **4 - Likely** | Probably will occur |
| **3 - Possible** | May occur |
| **2 - Unlikely** | Not expected |
| **1 - Rare** | Exceptional |

### Impact

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **5 - Critical** | Existential threat |
| **4 - Major** | Significant damage |
| **3 - Moderate** | Notable impact |
| **2 - Minor** | Limited impact |
| **1 - Negligible** | Minimal impact |

### Risk Rating

| Rating | Range | Action |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **Extreme** | 20-25 | Immediate action required |
| **High** | 12-19 | Senior management attention |
| **Medium** | 6-11 | Management responsibility |
| **Low** | 1-5 | Routine procedures |

---

## Risk Treatment

| Strategy | When Used |
|----------|-----------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk source |
| **Reduce** | Implement controls |
| **Transfer** | Insurance, contracts |
| **Accept** | Within risk appetite |

---

## Risk Governance

| Level | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Board** | Set risk appetite, oversight |
| **Management** | Implement framework, report |
| **Operational** | Identify and manage daily risks |
