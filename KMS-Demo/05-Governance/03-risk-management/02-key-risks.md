# Key Risks

## Purpose

This document identifies principal risks facing IMC Pelita Logistik and their mitigations.

---

## Strategic Risks

### Commodity Price Risk

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Risk** | Coal price volatility affects shipping demand |
| **Impact** | Revenue fluctuation |
| **Mitigation** | Long-term contracts (COA), diversification |

### Energy Transition Risk

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Risk** | Global shift away from thermal coal |
| **Impact** | Long-term demand decline |
| **Mitigation** | Diversify cargo types (nickel, clinker) |

### Competition Risk

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Risk** | Overcapacity in transshipment sector |
| **Impact** | Rate pressure, market share loss |
| **Mitigation** | Customer relationships, efficiency, quality |

## Operational Risks

### Fleet Availability

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Risk** | Vessel breakdown, off-hire |
| **Impact** | Lost revenue, customer dissatisfaction |
| **Mitigation** | PMS, adequate spares, dry dock planning |

### Safety Incidents

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Risk** | Accidents causing injury, damage, pollution |
| **Impact** | Human harm, liability, reputation |
| **Mitigation** | SMS, training, safety culture |

### Weather

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Risk** | Weather delays, monsoon season |
| **Impact** | Operational disruption |
| **Mitigation** | Planning, weather monitoring, contingency |
