# Board Structure

## Purpose

This document describes the dual-board governance structure of PT IMC Pelita Logistik Tbk.

---

## Dual-Board System

Indonesian corporate law mandates a two-tier board structure:

| Board | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Board of Commissioners** | Oversight and supervision |
| **Board of Directors** | Executive management |

---

## Board of Commissioners

### Role

- Supervise the Board of Directors
- Provide advice on company strategy
- Ensure compliance with laws and articles
- Protect shareholder interests

### Composition

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Minimum members** | 2 |
| **Independent commissioners** | At least 30% |
| **Chairman** | President Commissioner |

## Board of Directors

### Role

- Manage day-to-day operations
- Implement company strategy
- Represent company legally
- Report to Board of Commissioners

### Composition

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Minimum members** | 1 |
| **President Director** | Chief executive |

## Board Meetings

| Meeting | Frequency |
|---------|-----------|
| **Commissioners** | At least quarterly |
| **Directors** | Monthly or as needed |
| **Joint** | As needed |
