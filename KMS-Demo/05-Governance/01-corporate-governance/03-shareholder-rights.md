# Shareholder Rights

## Purpose

This document outlines shareholder rights and investor relations practices.

---

## Shareholder Rights

### Voting Rights

| Right | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **One share, one vote** | Equal voting rights |
| **AGM participation** | Attend and vote at meetings |
| **Proxy voting** | Can appoint proxy |

### Information Rights

| Right | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Annual report** | Receive audited financials |
| **Material information** | Timely disclosure |
| **AGM materials** | Agenda and proposals |

### Economic Rights

| Right | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Dividends** | Share in profits |
| **Pre-emptive rights** | Rights issues |
| **Residual claim** | On dissolution |

---

## Annual General Meeting (AGM)

### Required Agenda

| Item | Type |
|------|------|
| Annual report | Approval |
| Financial statements | Approval |
| Profit allocation | Approval |
| Auditor appointment | Approval |
| Commissioner/Director remuneration | Approval |

### Meeting Procedures

| Step | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Notice** | Published in advance |
| **Quorum** | Per articles of association |
| **Voting** | Show of hands or poll |
| **Minutes** | Recorded and published |

---

## Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM)

Called for matters requiring shareholder approval outside AGM:
- Major transactions
- Share capital changes
- Articles amendments
- Merger/acquisition

---

## Investor Relations

### Communication Channels

| Channel | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Annual report** | Comprehensive disclosure |
| **Quarterly reports** | Financial updates |
| **IDX filings** | Material announcements |
| **Website** | Investor information |
| **IR contact** | Direct communication |

### Dividend Policy

| Aspect | Policy |
|--------|--------|
| **Frequency** | Annual |
| **Payout** | Per profitability and cash position |
| **Recent yield** | ~9-10% (historical) |
