# Board Committees

## Purpose

This document describes the committees established under the Board of Commissioners.

---

## Audit Committee

### Purpose

Assist the Board of Commissioners in:
- Overseeing financial reporting
- Monitoring internal controls
- Overseeing external audit
- Ensuring compliance

### Composition

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Chairman** | Independent Commissioner |
| **Members** | External independent experts |
| **Minimum** | 3 members |

### Responsibilities

| Area | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Financial reporting** | Review accuracy and compliance |
| **Internal audit** | Oversee internal audit function |
| **External audit** | Recommend appointment, review scope |
| **Internal controls** | Monitor effectiveness |
| **Compliance** | Monitor regulatory compliance |

### Meetings

| Frequency | Minimum |
|-----------|---------|
| Regular | Quarterly |
| Special | As needed |

---

## Nomination & Remuneration Committee

### Purpose

Assist the Board of Commissioners in:
- Board composition
- Director/commissioner appointments
- Remuneration policy

### Composition

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Chairman** | Independent Commissioner |
| **Members** | Commissioners or independents |

### Responsibilities

| Area | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Nomination** | Identify and recommend candidates |
| **Evaluation** | Assess board performance |
| **Remuneration** | Recommend compensation policies |
| **Succession** | Succession planning |

---

## Committee Charters

Each committee operates under a charter that defines:
- Purpose and authority
- Composition requirements
- Roles and responsibilities
- Meeting procedures
- Reporting requirements
