# ISM Code Compliance

## Purpose

This document outlines PT IMC Pelita Logistik's compliance with the **International Safety Management (ISM) Code**, including our certification status, audit requirements, and key compliance elements.

---

## Scope

Applies to:
- All vessels operated under the company's Document of Compliance (DOC)
- Shore-based management responsible for safety management

## Non-Conformity Management

When audits identify non-conformities:

### Major Non-Conformity
A serious failure posing significant risk to safety, personnel, or environment, or absence of effective and systematic compliance with ISM Code.

**Response:** Corrective action within 3 months or certificate may be withdrawn.

### Non-Conformity (Minor)
An observed failure that does not pose immediate risk but indicates non-conformance with SMS.

**Response:** Corrective action tracked and closed before next audit.

### Observation
An area where improvement is recommended but is not a formal non-conformity.

**Response:** Consider for improvement, no mandatory action.
