# Transshipment Safety

Transshipment is the core of IMC Pelita Logistik's operations – the transfer of coal from barges to mother vessels via Floating Loading Facilities (FLF). This section covers the safety procedures for these critical operations.

---

## Transshipment Process Overview

```
┌──────────────┐     ┌─────────────────┐     ┌──────────────────┐
│   BARGE      │────►│     FLF         │────►│  MOTHER VESSEL   │
│  (from mine) │     │ (transshipment) │     │  (to export)     │
└──────────────┘     └─────────────────┘     └──────────────────┘
     River              Anchorage                Ocean
    Operation           Transfer               Voyage
```

---

## Key Hazards in Transshipment

| Hazard | Location | Consequence |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Collision** | Barge approach to FLF | Structural damage, injury |
| **Mooring failure** | Alongside FLF | Vessel drift, personnel injury |
| **Falling overboard** | Transfer between vessels | Drowning |
| **Struck by cargo** | Conveyor/grab areas | Serious injury, death |
| **Coal dust** | Loading zones | Respiratory illness, slip hazard |
| **Conveyor entanglement** | FLF deck | Amputation, death |
| **Crush injury** | Between vessels | Serious injury, death |

## Critical Safety Rules

### Before Operations:
- [ ] Weather conditions assessed and acceptable
- [ ] All personnel briefed on today's operations
- [ ] Communication established between all units
- [ ] PPE available and worn
- [ ] Emergency equipment checked

### During Operations:
- [ ] Maintain safe distances from moving equipment
- [ ] Stay out of bight of lines
- [ ] No crossing between vessels without gangway
- [ ] Continuous lookout maintained
- [ ] Stop operations if conditions deteriorate

### No-Go Criteria:
- Wind > 25 knots
- Wave height > 2 meters
- Visibility < 0.5 nm
- Night operations without adequate lighting
- Personnel not briefed

## Communication Protocol

| Communication | Channel/Method | Parties |
|---------------|----------------|---------|
| Barge approach | VHF Ch [designated] | Tug, FLF |
| Mooring coordination | VHF + visual | FLF deck, Barge crew |
| Cargo operations | Intercom / Radio | FLF control, Operators |
| Emergency | VHF Ch 16 + alarm | All units |
