# Hot Work Safety

## Purpose

This document provides safety procedures for hot work operations on IMC Pelita Logistik vessels and facilities.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All personnel on all vessels and facilities**

---

## Definition

**Hot work** is any work that produces sparks, flames, or heat sufficient to ignite flammable materials or atmospheres.

### Examples:
- Welding (arc, gas, stud)
- Cutting (oxy-fuel, plasma)
- Grinding and abrasive cutting
- Brazing and soldering
- Burning
- Heat shrinking

---

## Hot Work Hazards

| Hazard | Risk |
|--------|------|
| **Fire** | Sparks ignite flammables |
| **Explosion** | Flammable atmosphere ignition |
| **Burns** | Contact with hot materials |
| **Eye damage** | Arc flash, UV/IR radiation |
| **Fumes** | Toxic welding fumes |
| **Electric shock** | From welding equipment |

---

## Permit Requirements

**ALL hot work on board requires a Hot Work Permit.**

No exceptions.

### When Hot Work is Prohibited:

- ❌ During cargo operations with flammable cargo
- ❌ In gas-dangerous atmosphere (>1% LEL)
- ❌ Adjacent to spaces not gas-freed
- ❌ Without fire watch in place
- ❌ Without permit signed by Master/Chief Officer

---

## Pre-Hot Work Checklist

| Requirement | Checked |
|-------------|---------|
| Hot Work Permit issued | ☐ |
| Area inspected for fire hazards | ☐ |
| Combustibles removed or protected (11m radius) | ☐ |
| Atmosphere tested (if near tanks/holds) | ☐ |
| Fire extinguishers at work site | ☐ |
| Fire watch assigned | ☐ |
| Bridge and engine room notified | ☐ |
| Smoke detectors covered (with permit) | ☐ |
| Communication established | ☐ |

---

## Combustible Clearance

| Zone | Distance | Requirement |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Hot work area** | 11 meters (35 ft) radius | Remove all combustibles |
| **Below work area** | All decks | Cover openings, wet down if needed |
| **Adjacent compartments** | All boundaries | Check for combustibles on other side |

### If Combustibles Cannot Be Removed:

- Cover with fire-resistant blankets or shields
- Wet down with water
- Assign additional fire watch
- Consider if work can be done elsewhere

---

## Fire Watch

### Requirements:

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Personnel** | Trained, dedicated, no other duties |
| **Equipment** | Fire extinguisher, radio, watch |
| **Duration** | During work + 30 minutes after |
| **Coverage** | Both sides of bulkheads if applicable |
| **Authority** | Can stop work if unsafe |

### Fire Watch Duties:

- Watch for sparks and signs of fire
- Immediately extinguish any ignition
- Alert hot work team to hazards
- Sound alarm if fire develops
- Remain at post until released

---

## PPE for Hot Work

| PPE | Requirement |
|-----|-------------|
| **Welding helmet** | With correct shade lens |
| **Safety glasses** | Under helmet |
| **Welding gloves** | Long cuff, leather |
| **Fire-resistant clothing** | Cotton or FR material |
| **Safety boots** | Leather, cover by pants |
| **Ear protection** | If noise > 85 dB |
| **Respiratory** | If fumes/enclosure |

### Lens Shade Guide

| Process | Shade |
|---------|-------|
| Oxy-cutting | 4-6 |
| MIG welding | 10-12 |
| Arc welding (stick) | 10-14 |
| TIG welding | 10-14 |

---

## Welding Fumes

| Risk | Control |
|------|---------|
| Metal fume fever | Ventilation, respiratory protection |
| Coated electrodes | Use in well-ventilated areas |
| Galvanized steel | Zinc fumes – use fume extraction |
| Confined spaces | Forced ventilation + respiratory |

---

## Hot Work on Confined Spaces

Additional requirements:

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Gas-free certificate** | Before work begins |
| **Continuous atmosphere monitoring** | During work |
| **Forced ventilation** | Maintain fresh air supply |
| **Remove electrodes when breaks** | Prevent accidental arc |
| **Escape route clear** | At all times |

---

## Hot Work on Pipelines/Tanks

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Isolation** | Physically disconnected, not just valved |
| **Drained and cleaned** | No residue |
| **Gas-free certified** | <1% LEL confirmed |
| **Blanking** | Blank flanges in place |

---

## After Hot Work

| Action | Responsibility |
|--------|----------------|
| Fire watch continues 30 minutes | Fire watch |
| Inspect work area | Hot worker |
| Re-inspect 60 minutes later | Duty officer |
| Re-activate fire detection | Chief Officer |
| Close out permit | Authorizing officer |
