# Fatigue Management

## Purpose

This document provides guidance on managing fatigue to prevent accidents and ensure the health and safety of all IMC Pelita Logistik personnel.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All personnel on all vessels and facilities**

---

## Why Fatigue Matters

Fatigue impairs:
- Judgment and decision-making
- Reaction time
- Attention and concentration
- Memory
- Mood and communication

> **Fact:** Fatigue-related impairment at 17 hours without sleep is equivalent to a blood alcohol level of 0.05%.

### Maritime Incidents Linked to Fatigue:
- Collisions and groundings
- Cargo handling errors
- Falls and personal injuries
- Navigation errors
- Poor emergency response

---

## Regulatory Requirements

### STCW and MLC Hours of Work/Rest

| Requirement | Limit |
|-------------|-------|
| **Minimum rest** | 10 hours in any 24-hour period |
| **Rest period split** | Maximum 2 periods, one at least 6 hours |
| **Maximum hours** | 14 hours in any 24-hour period |
| **Maximum hours** | 72 hours in any 7-day period |

> **Note:** Exceptions exist for emergencies but must be documented.

---

## Signs of Fatigue

### Recognize in Yourself:
- Difficulty concentrating
- Yawning, heavy eyelids
- Feeling irritable
- Making mistakes on routine tasks
- Slow reaction time
- Microsleeps (nodding off)

### Recognize in Others:
- Poor coordination
- Slurred speech
- Unusual quiet or irritability
- Forgetting instructions
- Nodding off during watch

---

## Fatigue Risk Factors

| Factor | Increased Risk |
|--------|----------------|
| **Sleep debt** | Less than 7-8 hours sleep regularly |
| **Time of day** | 0200-0600 (circadian low point) |
| **Watch patterns** | Split watches, 6-on-6-off |
| **Workload** | Extended port operations |
| **Environment** | Noise, vibration, heat |
| **Health** | Illness, poor diet |
| **Personal** | Stress, family issues |

## Responsibilities

### Company

| Responsibility | Action |
|----------------|--------|
| Manning levels | Sufficient crew for safe operation |
| Work scheduling | Allow adequate rest |
| Monitoring | Review work/rest records |
| Culture | Support fatigue reporting |

### Master

| Responsibility | Action |
|----------------|--------|
| Watch schedules | Ensure compliance with regulations |
| Workload management | Distribute work fairly |
| Lead by example | Take rest, encourage others |
| Intervention | Relieve fatigued personnel |

### Individual

| Responsibility | Action |
|----------------|--------|
| Sleep management | Use off-duty time for rest |
| Report | Inform if too fatigued to work safely |
| Lifestyle | Limit caffeine, avoid alcohol |
| Fitness | Maintain physical health |

---

## Fatigue Mitigation Strategies

### During Operations

| Strategy | Application |
|----------|-------------|
| **Rotation** | Rotate personnel on repetitive tasks |
| **Breaks** | Regular short breaks during work |
| **Buddy system** | Partner watch to catch errors |
| **Limiting overtime** | Reduce extended hours where possible |
| **Critical hours** | Additional supervision 0200-0600 |

### Personal Strategies

| Strategy | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Power naps** | 20-30 min can improve alertness |
| **Caffeine timing** | Effective short-term, not before sleep |
| **Environment** | Cool, dark, quiet sleep space |
| **Routine** | Regular sleep schedule when possible |
| **Nutrition** | Light meals, stay hydrated |

---

## Work/Rest Recording

### Requirements:

| Record | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Daily log | Hours of work and rest |
| Signed | By individual and Master |
| Retained | On board for 3 months, ashore for 3 years |
| Inspection | Available for Port State Control |

### Non-Compliance:

If required rest cannot be provided:
1. Document reasons
2. Provide compensatory rest as soon as possible
3. Report to company
4. Do not allow it to become routine

---

## Reporting Fatigue Concerns

If you are too fatigued to work safely:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Inform your supervisor immediately |
| 2 | Do not start hazardous work |
| 3 | Request relief if on watch |
| 4 | Rest as directed |

> **No retaliation:** Reporting fatigue is encouraged and protected.
