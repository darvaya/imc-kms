# PPE Requirements

## Purpose

This document specifies Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) requirements by job role and activity for all IMC Pelita Logistik personnel.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All personnel on all vessels and facilities**

---

## PPE Hierarchy

PPE is the **last line of defense**. Always apply higher-level controls first:

1. ❌ **Eliminate** the hazard
2. 🔄 **Substitute** with less hazardous option
3. 🔧 **Engineer** controls (guards, barriers)
4. 📋 **Administrative** controls (procedures, training)
5. 🦺 **PPE** – Personal Protective Equipment

---

## Minimum PPE Standards

### All Personnel – General Requirements

| PPE | Standard | Condition |
|-----|----------|-----------|
| Safety footwear | Steel toe, slip-resistant | Always on deck/work areas |
| Work clothing | Long sleeves, fire-retardant where required | Always |
| High-visibility vest | Fluorescent with reflective strips | Deck work, cargo ops |

## PPE Specifications

### Head Protection

| Type | Use | Standard |
|------|-----|----------|
| Safety helmet (hard hat) | All deck and work areas | EN 397 / ANSI Z89.1 |
| Welding helmet | Hot work | EN 175 |
| Bump cap | Low-risk indoor areas | EN 812 |

### Eye Protection

| Type | Use |
|------|-----|
| Safety glasses | General work, grinding |
| Safety goggles | Chemical splash, dust |
| Welding shield | Arc welding (shade 10-14) |
| Face shield | Over glasses for grinding, chemical |

### Hearing Protection

| Type | NRR Rating | Use |
|------|------------|-----|
| Foam ear plugs | 25-33 dB | General noise areas |
| Ear muffs | 23-31 dB | High noise (engine room, prolonged) |
| Combined | Higher protection | Extreme noise |

**Noise action levels:**
- 85 dB(A) – Hearing protection available
- 90 dB(A) – Hearing protection mandatory

### Respiratory Protection

| Type | Protection | Use |
|------|------------|-----|
| Dust mask (P2/N95) | Particulates | Coal dust, general dust |
| Half-face respirator | Organic vapors | Painting, solvents |
| Full-face respirator | Higher protection | Chemical spills |
| BA/SCBA | IDLH atmospheres | Confined space rescue |

### Hand Protection

| Type | Use |
|------|-----|
| General work gloves | Rigging, general handling |
| Cut-resistant gloves | Sheet metal, wire |
| Welding gloves | Hot work |
| Chemical gloves (nitrile/neoprene) | Chemical handling |
| Heat-resistant gloves | Hot surfaces |

### Fall Protection

| Equipment | Use | Inspection |
|-----------|-----|------------|
| Full body harness | All work at heights | Before each use, formal annual |
| Double lanyard | Allow continuous attachment | Before each use |
| Self-retracting lifeline (SRL) | Extended work areas | Per manufacturer |
| Rescue kit | Standby for rescue | Annual |

---

## PPE Inspection

### Before Every Use:

| Check | What to Look For |
|-------|------------------|
| Hard hat | Cracks, dents, UV degradation |
| Safety glasses | Scratches, cracks, loose fit |
| Harness | Fraying, cuts, damaged stitching, corrosion |
| Gloves | Holes, contamination, loss of flexibility |
| Respirator | Seal integrity, cartridge life |

### Formal Inspection:

| Equipment | Frequency | By Whom |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Fall protection equipment | Annual | Competent person |
| Breathing apparatus | Per schedule | Manufacturer/class |
| Lifejackets | Annual | Officers |

---

## PPE Issuance & Care

### Issuance

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Record keeping | PPE register maintained |
| Size fitting | Correct size provided |
| Training | Use and care instruction |
| Replacement | On request or wear |

### Care and Storage

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Cleaning | Per manufacturer instructions |
| Storage | Dry, out of sunlight |
| Personal issue | Not shared where hygiene applies |
| Disposal | Per disposal procedures |

---

## Non-Compliance

Failure to use required PPE may result in:
- Work stopped until compliant
- Verbal warning
- Formal disciplinary action for repeat violations

> **Note:** Masters and supervisors are responsible for ensuring crew compliance.
