# Occupational Health & Safety (ISO 45001)

This section contains occupational health and safety procedures aligned with ISO 45001:2018 requirements, covering the day-to-day hazards faced by IMC Pelita Logistik personnel.

---

## ISO 45001:2018 Certification

PT IMC Pelita Logistik maintains **ISO 45001:2018 certification** for Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems, demonstrating our commitment to:

- Preventing work-related injury and ill health
- Providing safe and healthy workplaces
- Continually improving OH&S performance

---

## Key OHS Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Risk hierarchy** | Eliminate > Substitute > Engineer > Admin > PPE |
| **Worker participation** | Engage crew in hazard identification |
| **Leadership commitment** | Management visible support for safety |
| **Competence** | All workers trained for their tasks |
| **Continual improvement** | Learn from incidents and near misses |

## Work-Related Health Hazards

| Hazard | Exposure Source | Prevention |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| **Noise** | Engine room, equipment | Hearing protection, exposure limits |
| **Coal dust** | Cargo operations | Dust masks, dust suppression |
| **Chemical exposure** | Paints, fuels, cleaning agents | MSDS, PPE, ventilation |
| **Heat stress** | Tropical climate, engine room | Hydration, rest breaks |
| **Manual handling** | Lifting, carrying | Training, mechanical aids |
| **Fatigue** | Long hours, shift work | Work-rest schedules |

---

## Worker Health Monitoring

| Program | Frequency | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Pre-employment medical** | Once | Fitness for duty |
| **Periodic medical exam** | Annual (2 years for <40) | Ongoing fitness |
| **Audiometry** | Annual (noise-exposed) | Hearing conservation |
| **Drug & alcohol test** | Random / incident | Fitness for duty |
