# Drill Requirements

## Purpose

This document specifies emergency drill requirements to ensure crew readiness for emergencies.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All vessels**

---

## Regulatory Requirements

| Regulation | Requirement |
|------------|-------------|
| **SOLAS** | Fire and abandon ship drills |
| **ISM Code** | Regular drills for emergency procedures |
| **MARPOL** | Oil spill (SOPEP) drills |
| **Flag State** | Additional requirements may apply |

---

## Drill Schedule

### Monthly Drills (Minimum)

| Drill | Participants | Duration |
|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Fire and Abandon Ship** | All crew | ~1 hour |
| **Man Overboard (MOB)** | All crew | ~30 min |
| **Oil Spill (SOPEP)** | Designated crew | ~30 min |
| **Steering Failure** | Bridge/Engine | ~15 min |

### Quarterly Drills

| Drill | Participants |
|-------|--------------|
| **Confined Space Rescue** | Rescue team |
| **Medical Emergency (complex)** | Medical team |
| **Security (ISPS)** | Security team |

### Upon Crew Change

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| If >25% crew change | Drill within 24 hours of departure |
| New crew orientation | Familiarization with equipment |

---

## Fire and Abandon Ship Drill

### Objectives:
- Test emergency alarm system
- Practice muster and roll call
- Deploy firefighting equipment
- Prepare lifeboats/rafts
- Confirm crew know their duties

### Drill Elements:

| Element | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Alarm** | Sound general emergency alarm |
| **Muster** | All crew to muster stations |
| **Roll call** | Account for all personnel |
| **Lifejackets** | Don lifejackets |
| **Lifeboat** | Swing out, don't launch (or launch if practicable) |
| **Fire equipment** | Demonstrate use of extinguishers, hoses |
| **Debrief** | Review performance, improvements |

---

## Man Overboard (MOB) Drill

### Objectives:
- Practice immediate response
- Test MOB equipment
- Practice recovery maneuvers

### Drill Elements:

| Element | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Alert** | Simulate MOB sighting |
| **Lifebuoy** | Deploy lifebuoy with light |
| **Maneuver** | Execute Williamson turn |
| **Rescue boat** | Prepare/launch rescue boat |
| **Recovery** | Practice casualty recovery |
| **First aid** | Simulate medical response |

---

## Oil Spill (SOPEP) Drill

### Objectives:
- Test SOPEP equipment readiness
- Practice containment procedures
- Review notification procedures

### Drill Elements:

| Element | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Simulate spill** | Scenario-based |
| **Deploy equipment** | Absorbents, booms |
| **Containment** | Plug scuppers, deploy boom |
| **Notification** | Practice reporting chain |
| **Clean-up** | Demonstrate procedures |
| **Documentation** | Complete drill record |

---

## Drill Recording

All drills must be recorded in the **Official Log Book** with:

| Information | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Date and time | When drill conducted |
| Type of drill | Fire, abandon ship, MOB, etc. |
| Participants | All crew or specific team |
| Equipment used | What was tested |
| Observations | Issues identified |
| Corrective actions | Follow-up needed |
| Master signature | Verification |

---

## Drill Effectiveness

### Post-Drill Debrief

After each drill:
1. Gather crew feedback
2. Discuss what went well
3. Identify problems
4. Agree corrective actions
5. Document lessons learned

### Performance Indicators

| Indicator | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Muster time | <5 minutes |
| Roll call accuracy | 100% |
| Equipment function | 100% |
| Crew knowledge | All know duties |
