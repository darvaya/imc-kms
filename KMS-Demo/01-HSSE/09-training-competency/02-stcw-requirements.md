# STCW Requirements

## Purpose

This document summarizes STCW (Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping) requirements for seafarers on IMC Pelita Logistik vessels.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All seafarers on international voyaging vessels**

---

## What is STCW?

The **International Convention on Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping for Seafarers (STCW)** sets minimum qualification standards for masters, officers, and watch personnel on seagoing merchant ships.

| Convention | Year | Status |
|------------|------|--------|
| STCW | 1978 | Original |
| STCW 95 | 1995 | Major revision |
| STCW Manila | 2010 | Current amendments |

---

## Basic Safety Training (BST)

**Required for:** All seafarers

| Module | Content | Initial/Refresher |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| **Personal Survival Techniques** | Abandonment, survival at sea | Initial + 5yr |
| **Fire Prevention and Firefighting** | Basic firefighting | Initial + 5yr |
| **Elementary First Aid** | Basic medical | Initial + 5yr |
| **Personal Safety and Social Responsibility** | Ship safety, environmental protection | Initial only |

---

## Advanced Training

### Advanced Firefighting (STCW VI/3)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Required for** | Officers, firefighting party leaders |
| **Content** | Fire control, rescue, leading firefighting teams |
| **Refresher** | Every 5 years |

### Medical Training

| Level | For Whom |
|-------|----------|
| **Medical First Aid** | Officers designated for medical duties |
| **Medical Care** | Masters, designated officers on ships without doctor |

### Proficiency in Survival Craft (STCW VI/2)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Required for** | Officers, persons in charge of survival craft |
| **Content** | Lifeboat/raft operation, rescue boats |
| **Refresher** | Every 5 years |

## Officer Certificates

### Deck Officers

| Certificate | Voyage Type | Min. Seagoing Service |
|-------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **OOW (III/1)** | Unlimited | 12 months as trainee |
| **Chief Mate (II/2)** | Unlimited | 12 months as OOW |
| **Master (II/2)** | Unlimited | 36 months as officer |

### Engine Officers

| Certificate | Engine kW | Min. Seagoing Service |
|-------------|-----------|----------------------|
| **OICEW (III/1)** | Unlimited | 12 months as trainee |
| **Second Engineer (III/2)** | Unlimited | 12 months as OICEW |
| **Chief Engineer (III/2)** | Unlimited | 36 months as officer |

## Certificate Validation

| Check | Method |
|-------|--------|
| **Authenticity** | Verify with issuing flag state |
| **Validity** | Check expiry date |
| **STCW endorsement** | Required for foreign certificates |
| **Medical fitness** | Valid medical certificate |

---

## Work/Rest Hour Compliance

Per STCW, all watchkeeping personnel must observe:

| Requirement | Limit |
|-------------|-------|
| Minimum rest | 10 hours in any 24-hour period |
| Rest period split | Max 2 periods, one ≥6 hours |
| Maximum hours | 14 hours in any 24-hour period |
| Maximum hours | 72 hours in any 7-day period |
