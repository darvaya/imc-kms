# Training & Competency

This section covers training requirements and competency management to ensure all personnel are qualified and capable of performing their duties safely.

---

## Training Philosophy

> **"Competent people make safe operations possible"**

We invest in training to:
- Prevent incidents through skilled personnel
- Meet regulatory requirements
- Develop our workforce
- Maintain competitive advantage

## Competency Framework

| Level | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Awareness** | Understands basics, can recognize hazards |
| **Knowledge** | Understands principles and procedures |
| **Competent** | Can perform safely with oversight |
| **Proficient** | Can perform independently |
| **Expert** | Can train others, solve complex problems |

---

## Training Types

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **Mandatory** | STCW basic training, company induction |
| **Job-specific** | Equipment operation, cargo handling |
| **Refresher** | Re-certification, skills maintenance |
| **Development** | Leadership, specialized skills |
| **Emergency response** | Drills, firefighting, first aid |
