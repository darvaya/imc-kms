# Waste Management

## Purpose

This document provides procedures for proper waste management on IMC Pelita Logistik vessels in compliance with MARPOL Annex V.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All vessel types**

---

## Garbage Categories (MARPOL Annex V)

| Category | Examples | Discharge at Sea | Discharge in Port |
|----------|----------|------------------|-------------------|
| **A - Plastics** | Plastic bags, bottles, rope | ❌ Prohibited | ✅ Ashore only |
| **B - Food waste** | Galley scraps | Conditional* | ✅ Ashore |
| **C - Domestic waste** | Paper, glass, metal | ❌ Prohibited | ✅ Ashore |
| **D - Cooking oil** | Used cooking oil | ❌ Prohibited | ✅ Ashore |
| **E - Incinerator ash** | Burned waste ash | Conditional** | ✅ Ashore |
| **F - Operational waste** | Maintenance waste | ❌ Prohibited | ✅ Ashore |
| **G - Animal carcasses** | Dead animals | Conditional*** | ✅ Ashore |
| **H - Fishing gear** | Nets, lines | ❌ Prohibited | ✅ Ashore |
| **I - E-waste** | Electronics | ❌ Prohibited | ✅ Ashore |
| **J - Cargo residues (non-HME)** | Coal residue | Conditional | ✅ Ashore |

*Food waste: >12nm from shore, ground to <25mm
**Ash: >12nm from shore
***Animal carcasses: As far from shore as possible

---

## Special Areas – No Discharge

In Special Areas (Baltic, Mediterranean, etc.), **all discharge is prohibited** except:
- Comminuted food waste >12nm from shore

> Indonesian waters include the Java Sea – check current regulations.

---

## Shipboard Garbage Management Plan

Every vessel must have a Garbage Management Plan covering:

1. Designated person for garbage management
2. Garbage collection procedures
3. Garbage storage locations
4. Garbage processing equipment (compactor, incinerator)
5. Garbage discharge procedures
6. Record keeping

---

## Garbage Handling Procedures

### Collection

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Separate garbage at source into categories |
| 2 | Use labeled bins for each category |
| 3 | Collect regularly to prevent overflow |
| 4 | Transport to designated storage area |

### Labeled Bins Required

| Bin Color | Category |
|-----------|----------|
| Blue | Plastics |
| Green | Food waste |
| Yellow | Recyclables (paper, glass, metal) |
| Red | Hazardous (batteries, chemicals) |
| Black | General/domestic |

### Storage

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Location | Designated garbage storage area on deck |
| Protection | Covered to prevent blowing overboard |
| Capacity | Sufficient for voyage + contingency |
| Hazardous segregation | Chemicals, oily waste separate |

---

## Garbage Discharge to Shore

### Procedure

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Notify port reception facility |
| 2 | Complete garbage reception form |
| 3 | Witness transfer to shore |
| 4 | Obtain receipt |
| 5 | Record in Garbage Record Book |

---

## Garbage Record Book

### Required Entries

| Entry | When |
|-------|------|
| Discharge to sea | Each occurrence (where permitted) |
| Discharge to shore | Each occurrence |
| Incineration | Each occurrence |
| Accidental loss | When discovered |

### Retention

- Keep on board for 2 years from last entry
- Available for Port State Control inspection

---

## Oily Waste Management

| Waste Type | Handling |
|------------|----------|
| **Bilge water** | Oily water separator to 15 ppm, or retain |
| **Sludge** | Transfer ashore only |
| **Oily rags** | Incinerate (where permitted) or ashore |
| **Used oil** | Transfer ashore via licensed receiver |

> All oily waste transfers recorded in Oil Record Book Part I.

---

## Hazardous Waste

| Waste Type | Handling |
|------------|----------|
| Batteries | Store in acid-proof container, dispose ashore |
| Fluorescent tubes | Store unbroken, dispose ashore |
| Paint/solvents | Store in designated locker, dispose ashore |
| Medical waste | Incinerate or dispose ashore as medical waste |
| Electronics (e-waste) | Dispose at approved facility ashore |

---

## Incinerator Use

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Permitted materials | Cardboard, paper, food waste, oily rags |
| Prohibited | Plastics containing halogens, PCBs |
| Location | >12nm from shore for ash disposal |
| Records | Log incinerator use in Garbage Record Book |
