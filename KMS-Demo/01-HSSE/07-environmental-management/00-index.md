# Environmental Management (ISO 14001)

This section covers environmental protection procedures aligned with ISO 14001:2015 requirements to minimize the environmental impact of IMC Pelita Logistik operations.

---

## ISO 14001:2015 Certification

PT IMC Pelita Logistik maintains **ISO 14001:2015 certification** for Environmental Management Systems, demonstrating our commitment to:

- Preventing pollution
- Complying with environmental regulations
- Continually improving environmental performance

---

## Environmental Policy Commitment

We are committed to:

1. **Preventing pollution** from our operations
2. **Minimizing waste** and maximizing recycling
3. **Reducing emissions** from our fleet
4. **Protecting marine ecosystems** where we operate
5. **Complying with MARPOL** and all applicable regulations

---

## Environmental Aspects

| Aspect | Impact | Control |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **Oil spills** | Marine pollution | SOPEP, containment, training |
| **Garbage disposal** | Marine litter | Garbage Management Plan |
| **Air emissions** | Climate change, air quality | Fuel management, maintenance |
| **Ballast water** | Invasive species | BWM Plan, treatment/exchange |
| **Sewage** | Water quality | Treatment, discharge rules |
| **Coal dust** | Air quality, marine sediment | Dust suppression |
| **Bunker operations** | Spill risk | Pre-transfer checklists |

## MARPOL Annexes Quick Reference

| Annex | Subject | Key Requirements |
|-------|---------|------------------|
| **I** | Oil | SOPEP, Oil Record Book |
| **II** | Noxious Liquids | Procedures and Record Book |
| **III** | Harmful Substances in Package | Labeling, documentation |
| **IV** | Sewage | Treatment or holding |
| **V** | Garbage | Garbage Management Plan |
| **VI** | Air Emissions | Fuel sulfur limits, NOx controls |
