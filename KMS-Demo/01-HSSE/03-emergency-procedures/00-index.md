# Emergency Procedures

This section contains emergency response procedures for all IMC Pelita Logistik vessels. All personnel must be familiar with these procedures and participate in regular drills.

---

## Emergency Response Philosophy

> **"Every second counts in an emergency. Know your role, know your equipment, and act decisively."**

### Key Principles

1. **Preserve life** – Human safety is the first priority
2. **Raise the alarm** – Immediate notification allows coordinated response
3. **Contain the situation** – Prevent escalation
4. **Communicate clearly** – Keep all parties informed
5. **Request assistance** – Don't hesitate to call for help

---

## Emergency Alarm Signals

| Signal | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Continuous** (7+ short blasts + 1 long) | General Emergency / Abandon Ship | Proceed to muster station with lifejacket |
| **Continuous ringing** | Fire | Proceed to muster station, fire teams respond |
| **3 long blasts** | Man Overboard | MOB team deploy, maintain visual |
| **Intermittent** | Drill only | Proceed as if real emergency |

---

## Emergency Stations

All crew members are assigned emergency duties as posted on the **Muster List**.

| Role | Location | Responsibility |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **On-Scene Commander** | Incident location | Direct immediate response |
| **Bridge Team** | Navigation Bridge | Communication, coordination, navigation |
| **Fire Team** | Fire station | Firefighting, boundary cooling |
| **Medical Team** | Medical station | First aid, prepare for evacuation |
| **Engineering Team** | Engine Control Room | Systems support, isolation |
| **Support Team** | Muster station | Equipment, assist casualties |

## Drill Requirements

| Drill Type | Frequency | Participants |
|------------|-----------|--------------|
| Fire & Abandon Ship | Monthly | All crew |
| Man Overboard | Monthly | All crew |
| Oil Spill (SOPEP) | Monthly | Designated personnel |
| Confined Space Rescue | Quarterly | Rescue team |
| Medical Emergency | Quarterly | Medical team |

> **Note:** Drills must be recorded in the vessel's official log book.
