# Coal Cargo Safety

Coal is the primary cargo for IMC Pelita Logistik. This section covers the unique hazards of coal cargo and the procedures to manage them safely.

---

## Coal Cargo Hazards Overview

| Hazard | Risk | Consequence |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **Self-heating / Spontaneous Combustion** | Coal oxidizes and generates heat | Cargo fire |
| **Methane Emission** | Coal releases flammable gas | Explosion in enclosed space |
| **Oxygen Depletion** | Coal absorbs oxygen | Asphyxiation in hold |
| **Coal Dust** | Explosive when airborne | Dust explosion, respiratory illness |
| **Cargo Shift** | Improperly trimmed coal moves | Vessel capsize |

---

## Critical Safety Measures

### Before Loading
- Obtain cargo declaration with coal properties
- Check for history of self-heating
- Ensure hold is clean and dry
- Test and calibrate monitoring equipment

### During Loading
- Monitor for dust emissions
- Ensure proper trimming
- No hot work during cargo operations
- Restrict smoking and open flames

### During Voyage
- Monitor cargo temperature
- Monitor hold atmosphere (O2, CH4, CO)
- Maintain proper ventilation strategy
- No entry to holds without permit

### During Discharge
- Continue monitoring until empty
- Dust suppression during grab operations
- Clean holds after discharge
