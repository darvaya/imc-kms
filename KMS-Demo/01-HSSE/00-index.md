# HSSE - Health, Safety, Security & Environment

Welcome to the **Health, Safety, Security, and Environment (HSSE)** collection for PT IMC Pelita Logistik. This collection contains all policies, procedures, and guidelines that ensure the safety of our people, protection of the environment, and compliance with maritime regulations.

---

## Our Commitment

At IMC Pelita Logistik, we believe that **no task is so urgent that it cannot be done safely**. Our HSSE management system is built on the COMPASS values, particularly **Mindfulness** – consistently considering the impact of our decisions on others and the environment.

## Vessel Type Applicability

Our procedures are tailored to the unique hazards of each vessel type:

| Vessel Type | Description | Key Hazards |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **FLF** | Floating Loading Facilities | Conveyor operations, high-volume transfers, crane operations |
| **T&B** | Tug & Barge | River navigation, towing operations, coupling/decoupling |
| **MV** | Motor Vessels (Bulk Carriers) | Ocean navigation, international regulations, Port State Control |
