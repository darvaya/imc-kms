# Indonesian Flag State Requirements

## Purpose

This document summarizes key Indonesian maritime regulatory requirements applicable to IMC Pelita Logistik vessels.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All Indonesian-flagged vessels**

---

## Regulatory Authority

| Authority | Role |
|-----------|------|
| **Ministry of Transportation (Kemenhub)** | Overall maritime policy |
| **Directorate General of Sea Transportation (DGST)** | Flag State administration |
| **Harbor Master (Syahbandar)** | Port-level enforcement |
| **Indonesian Classification Bureau (BKI)** | Class surveys (if applicable) |

---

## Key Regulations

### Shipping Law (UU 17/2008)

Primary legislation governing maritime activities in Indonesia.

| Chapter | Subject |
|---------|---------|
| Ship registration | Indonesian flag requirements |
| Safety | Safety standards |
| Crew | Manning requirements |
| Environmental | Pollution prevention |

### Government Regulations (PP)

| Regulation | Subject |
|------------|---------|
| PP 51/2002 | Ship registration |
| PP 20/2010 | Water transport |
| PP 21/2010 | Maritime environmental protection |

---

## Manning Requirements

Indonesian vessels must comply with:
- Minimum Safe Manning Document
- Indonesian seafarer certificates or recognized certificates
- Crew ratio requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Indonesian crew ratio | Typically minimum 80% |
| Foreign crew | Work permit required |
| Certificate recognition | Indonesian CoC or Flag State endorsement |

---

## Documentation Requirements

### Vessel Documents (examples)

| Document | Issuing Authority |
|----------|-------------------|
| Certificate of Indonesian Registry | DGST |
| Minimum Safe Manning | DGST |
| Safety certificates (SOLAS) | DGST or RO |
| Load Line Certificate | Class Society |
| ISM/ISPS Certificates | DGST or RO |
| Radio certificates | DGST |

### Crew Documents

| Document | Details |
|----------|---------|
| CoC (Certificate of Competency) | Indonesian or endorsed |
| Seaman's Book | All Indonesian seafarers |
| Medical Certificate | Valid fitness certificate |
| Training certificates | STCW training |

---

## Inspection Regime

| Inspection | Authority | Frequency |
|------------|-----------|-----------|
| Annual safety inspection | DGST/Class | Annual |
| Port clearance | Syahbandar | Each departure |
| Random inspection | DGST | As determined |

---

## Reporting Requirements

| Event | Report To | Timeline |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Accident/incident | DGST, KNKT | Immediately |
| Pollution | DGST, Port Authority | Immediately |
| Security incident | DGST | Immediately |
| Change of ownership | DGST | As required |

### KNKT

The **Komite Nasional Keselamatan Transportasi (KNKT)** is the Indonesian Transport Safety Committee that investigates serious maritime incidents.
