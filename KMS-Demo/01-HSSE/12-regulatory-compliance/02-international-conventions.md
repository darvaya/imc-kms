# International Conventions

## Purpose

This document provides an overview of key international maritime conventions applicable to IMC Pelita Logistik operations.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All vessels engaged in international voyages**

---

## IMO Conventions

The **International Maritime Organization (IMO)** develops international maritime standards.

### SOLAS – Safety of Life at Sea

| Chapter | Subject |
|---------|---------|
| I | General provisions |
| II-1 | Construction (subdivision, stability) |
| II-2 | Fire protection |
| III | Life-saving appliances |
| IV | Radio communications |
| V | Safety of navigation |
| VI | Carriage of cargoes |
| VII | Dangerous goods |
| VIII | Nuclear ships |
| IX | **ISM Code** |
| X | Safety measures for high-speed craft |
| XI-1 | Special measures to enhance safety |
| XI-2 | **ISPS Code** |
| XII | Bulk carrier safety |

---

### MARPOL – Prevention of Pollution

| Annex | Subject | Key Requirements |
|-------|---------|------------------|
| I | Oil pollution | SOPEP, Oil Record Book, OWS |
| II | Noxious liquid substances | Cargo residues |
| III | Harmful substances in package | Labeling, stowage |
| IV | Sewage | Treatment, discharge rules |
| V | Garbage | Garbage Management Plan |
| VI | Air emissions | Sulfur limits, NOx, energy efficiency |

---

### STCW – Training, Certification, Watchkeeping

Standards for seafarer qualifications (see [STCW Requirements](../09-training-competency/02-stcw-requirements.md)).

---

### Load Line Convention

Minimum freeboard requirements based on vessel type and service.

---

### IMSBC Code – Solid Bulk Cargoes

Critical for coal operations (see [Coal Cargo Safety](../04-coal-cargo-safety/00-index.md)).

| Chapter | Subject |
|---------|---------|
| Cargo declaration | Shipper's responsibilities |
| Cargo hazards | Self-heating, liquefaction |
| Individual cargo schedules | Specific requirements per cargo |

---

## ILO Conventions

The **International Labour Organization (ILO)** sets maritime labour standards.

### MLC 2006 – Maritime Labour Convention

| Title | Subject |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Minimum requirements (age, medical, training) |
| 2 | Conditions of employment (agreements, wages, hours) |
| 3 | Accommodation, food, medical care |
| 4 | Health protection, welfare, social security |
| 5 | Compliance and enforcement |

**Key Requirements:**
- Seafarer Employment Agreement
- Maximum work hours / minimum rest
- Minimum accommodation standards
- Repatriation rights
- Medical care provisions

---

## BWM Convention – Ballast Water Management

Requirements to prevent spread of invasive species through ballast water.

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| BWM Plan | Required for each vessel |
| Treatment | D-1 (exchange) or D-2 (treatment) standard |
| Record Book | Ballast Water Record Book |
