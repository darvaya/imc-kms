# Incident Reporting Procedures

## Purpose

This document provides the procedures for reporting incidents on IMC Pelita Logistik vessels and facilities.

---

## Scope

Applies to: **All personnel on all vessels and facilities**

---

## What Must Be Reported

| Report | Examples |
|--------|----------|
| **All injuries** | From first aid to fatality |
| **All near misses** | "Almost" incidents |
| **Property damage** | Vessel, cargo, equipment |
| **Environmental incidents** | Spills, pollution |
| **Security incidents** | Theft, stowaway, piracy |
| **Occupational illness** | Work-related health issues |
| **Non-conformities** | Audit findings, inspection defects |

---

## Initial Verbal Report

Immediately after an incident, verbally report to:

| Severity | Report To | Timeline |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| All incidents | Immediate supervisor | Immediately |
| Serious injury | Master/Captain | Immediately |
| Fatality | Master → DPA → Management | Immediately |
| Major environmental | Master → DPA | Immediately |

### Information to Provide:

- What happened
- Where and when
- Who was involved
- Any injuries
- Immediate response taken

---

## Written Incident Report

### Who Completes:

- Person involved (if able)
- Supervisor (if person unable)
- Witnesses (separate statements)

### Timeline:

| Severity | Written Report Due |
|----------|--------------------|
| Fatality/Major | Within 24 hours |
| LTI | Within 24 hours |
| MTC/RWC | Within 48 hours |
| FAC/Near Miss | Within 72 hours |

### Report Contents:

| Section | Details Required |
|---------|------------------|
| **1. Details** | Date, time, location, persons involved |
| **2. Description** | What happened (factual, objective) |
| **3. Injuries** | Nature and severity |
| **4. Damage** | Equipment, property, environmental |
| **5. Immediate cause** | What directly caused it |
| **6. Contributing factors** | What allowed it to happen |
| **7. Immediate actions** | What was done in response |
| **8. Witnesses** | Names of those who saw it |
| **9. Photos/diagrams** | Visual evidence |
| **10. Recommendations** | Suggested actions to prevent recurrence |

## Near Miss Reporting

### Encouraged Behavior

Near miss reporting is:
- ✅ Encouraged and valued
- ✅ Anonymous option available
- ✅ No punishment for honest reporting
- ✅ Recognized and thanked

### What Qualifies:

Any situation where **harm could have occurred** but did not:
- Dropped objects that missed people
- Slips that didn't result in falls
- Equipment that nearly failed
- Unsafe conditions observed
