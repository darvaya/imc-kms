# Audits & Inspections

This section covers audit and inspection requirements to verify compliance with HSSE standards and identify opportunities for improvement.

---

## Audit Types

| Type | Conducted By | Frequency |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| **Internal SMS Audit** | HSSE Manager / Internal Auditor | Annual |
| **External ISM Audit** | Classification Society | Annual + 5-year renewal |
| **ISPS Audit** | Classification Society | Per ISM cycle |
| **Port State Control** | Port State Authority | Random |
| **Client Vetting** | Charterers/Clients | As required |
| **Management Review** | Senior Management | Annual |

         │
         ▼

         │
         ▼

         │
         ▼
